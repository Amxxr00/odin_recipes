SMOKED JOLLOF

SERVING

2-4

PREP TIME

5-10 minutes

COOK TIME

30-40 minute

  • This quick, streamlined recipe makes an incredibly tasty side dish and is perfect for when you don't feel like chopping lots of vegetables.

    • INGREDIENTS

    1. 2 Tbsp Oil t
    2. 1 Tbsp finely diced onion (optional)
    3. 1 Tbsp Jollof Rice West African Seasoning
    4. 1 Tbsp tomato paste
    5. 1 cup long-grain rice (washed & drained) 1
    6. 1 cup water or stock
    7. iru
    8. salina crystal salt

    DIRECTIONS

    • If using onion, sweat in oil on low-medium heat for 1-2 minutes. If using Iru, add 2-3 beans and cook for 1 minute to release the oils and flavor. Add the tomato paste to the oil and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring
    • Add in the Jollof Seasoning while stirring, to bloom for 1 minute
    • Add the washed rice and stir for 2-3 minutes, until the rice is evenly coated.
    • Add in water or stock, salt to taste, stir, and cover the pot.
    • Turn the burner to low heat and allow the rice to cook for 20 to 30 minutes.
    • Salt to taste and serve as is, or with your desired protein and vegetables.
