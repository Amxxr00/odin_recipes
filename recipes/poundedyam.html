TASTIA SWALLOW

DESCRIPTION

The swallow is a staple food in many African countries, particularly in West Africa. It is a thick, starchy dough made from various ingredients such as cassava, yams, plantains, or maize. The dough is typically boiled or steamed and then pounded to achieve a smooth texture.

INGREDIENTS

  • cassava
  • yams
  • plantains
  • maize/corn

STEPS

  1. boil your water till it is hot
  2. then you pour in your dough
  3. stir carefully to avoid lumps
  4. then you close it to allow it to get thick
  5. then you garnish it
