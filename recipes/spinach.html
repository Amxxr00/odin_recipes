BEST SPINACH DIP

DESCRIPTION

Spinach dim sum: Combine finely chopped spinach, minced pork, ginger, and garlic, wrap in dumpling wrappers, and steam until cooked through.

INGREDIENTS

  • spinach
  • garlic (minced)
  • salt and onion
  • Sour cream or mayonnaise
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)

STEPS IN PREPARATION

  1. Cook the Spinach: If using fresh spinach, blanch it in boiling water for a minute or until wilted. If using frozen spinach, thaw and drain it well.
  2. Combine Ingredients: In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked spinach, cream cheese, sour cream or mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, finely chopped onion (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Season to Taste: Taste the mixture and adjust seasoning if needed, adding more salt, pepper, or garlic according to your preference.
  4. Chill (Optional): For best flavor, refrigerate the dip for at least an hour to allow the flavors to meld together. This step is optional but recommended.
  5. Serve: Transfer the spinach dip to a serving bowl. It can be served cold, at room temperature, or warmed depending on preference. Serve with bread slices, crackers, tortilla chips, or vegetables for dipping.
  6. Garnish (Optional): You can garnish the spinach dip with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, chopped fresh herbs (like parsley or chives), or a drizzle of olive oil before serving.
